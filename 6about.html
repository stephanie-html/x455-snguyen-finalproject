HELLO!

Stephanie is a freelance illustrator based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

After a short stint in public accounting, Stephanie realized she would rather be looking at the colors of paint than the colors of excel sheet tabs. She now spends her days illustrating children's books.

FAQ

I went to San Jose State University where I got a BS degree in Accounting Information Systems. I merely tolerated the study of accounting, but I didn't realize the consequences of not pursuing something you actually enjoy doing until I started working as a public auditor.

I've spent the majority of my life appreciating good art, but one day, 3 months after I spontaneously quit my job as an auditor, I decided that I would become the one making the art. I owe a great deal of my art education to Schoolism and the internet at large.

I primarily use Procreate on the iPad. Occassionally I will venture off and use Photoshop. I have to admit that I don't do a ton of traditional painting.
